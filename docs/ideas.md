# Ideas

Blue sky ideas and inspirations. Not committed to, not in scope — just worth remembering.

## UX Inspirations

- **Duolingo notification UX** — Duolingo prompts users to disable other apps' notifications. Could the app gently encourage presence by suggesting users mute other social apps while using it?
- **Locket Rollcall** — Prompts users to share a collection of favorite photos from the past week with closest friends. Popular with Gen Alpha. Could inspire a periodic sharing ritual.

## Monetization Ideas

- **Donation-based model** — No ads, no data mining. Users donate voluntarily.
- **Donor rewards** — Subtle recognition for donors: special banners, tags, or cosmetic touches. Must not create hierarchy or status signaling — that contradicts the product philosophy.

## Growth Ideas

- **Invite-only launch** — Users can only get access if invited. Creates intentional, clustered real-world adoption. Builds culture before scale.
- **Friend-only onboarding** — You can only join if an existing user invites you. No public signup.

## Feature Ideas (Post-MVP)

- **Notification quieting** — The app could suggest turning off notifications from other social apps, reinforcing intentional use.
- **Collab posts** — If two people connect/reconnect and either posts shortly after, the moment could become a collab post — appearing on both people's feeds as a shared moment. Two perspectives of the same real-world interaction.
